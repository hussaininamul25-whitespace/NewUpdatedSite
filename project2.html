Shaanze

(Introduction)

Based in Guwahati, Assam, this premium gifting boutique specializes in silver-plated, brass, and metal décor that merges heritage-inspired craftsmanship with modern luxury. Each piece transforms traditional metalwork into refined keepsakes—gifts that honor legacy while embodying contemporary elegance.

(Challenges)

Shaanze required a cohesive brand identity and logo that felt luxurious yet approachable. The challenge was to strike a delicate balance between their heritage-inspired metal craftsmanship and a modern, minimalist aesthetic.
